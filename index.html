Products and timetables

  • Protection: Begin analysis of deforestation drivers to understand the role of agriculture vs. charcoal in forest cover reduction.

  • Production: Develop land use data from Phase I into a tool that can be used to better identify land that is actually available (prototype by end of Phase II).

Geographic emphasis: Manica province

  • Suitable for fruit cultivation.
  • Rapid deforestation
  • Partners in area (TechnoServ, AgDevCo).
  • Infrastructure access (Beira Corridor)

Protection emphasis: deforestation analysis

Data from TIA, satellite images.
Question: is deforestation faster where farmers report they are engaged in agriculture? If so, what type? What about charcoal and logging?
Method: econometric analysis.

Production emphasis: Agriculture Investment Tool

Objective: tool for agricultural investors from Phase 1 Data.
Structure (current concept) - four part decision tool:
  1. Physical suitability (good for crops, no high-value forests, not protected area)
  2. Infrastructure: port access + electricity (if needed)
  3. Government engagement: relevant officials and local organizations
  4. Action plan - timeline for contacting relevant government bodies, organizing community sessions, et cetera.
Prototype by December 2014.

Post-2014: top priorities

  • Completion of deforestation study.
  • Further development of toolkit pilot (pending funding).
  • Permanent partnerships with new government.
  • Decision about long-term location and nature of Southern Africa program.
